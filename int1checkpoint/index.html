About Me

What is My Favorite Sport?

My favorite sport is volleyball. I have been playing volleyball for 5 years. I've played club volleyball for 4 years and gone to the Girls Junior National Championships 3 times. To go to Junior Nationals you have to earn a bid. To earn a bid, you have to be one of the top teams in your region or win a national qualifier.
